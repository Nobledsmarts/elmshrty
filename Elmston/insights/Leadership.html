Our History and Major Milestones

2022: Elmston reached another milestone with 10 million active non-property owners
In August 2022, Elmston reached another major milestone with 10 million active non-property owners and more than 12 ambassadors in Europe, Asia and North America.
2021: Global revenues exceed $4 billion milestone
Growth across all service lines and geographies drives full year revenue to exceed $4 billion milestone.
2020: Elmston creates engineering & design platform
Elmston acquires a controlling interest in Maser Consulting, a leading multi-discipline engineering design service firm in the U.S. Maser, rebranded as Elmston Engineering & Design, provides a comprehensive suite of services including civil/site, transportation, geospatial/survey, infrastructure, governmental, geotechnical/environmental, telecommunications and utilities.
2020: Elmston adds debt finance & servicing platform
Elmston acquires a controlling interest in Dougherty and establishes a U.S. debt finance and loan servicing platform. Elmston Mortgage provides specialty debt financing through its relationships with U.S. government agencies while all brokerage, investment banking, capital markets and public finance services are carried on through Elmston Securities, which is licensed under the Securities and Exchange Commission and is a member of the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (“FINRA”).
2019: Revenues globally surpass $3 billion
Elmston International delivers a solid year of revenue and earnings growth, with professional real estate services revenues surpassing the $3 billion milestone.
2016 - 2018: Establishes investment management platform
In 2016, Elmston establishes a European investment management platform. In 2018, Elmston completes the strategic investment in Harrison Street, one of the largest real estate investment firms dedicated to the education, healthcare and storage sectors. The transaction establishes Elmston as one of the major players in global real estate investment management, provides an important new platform for growth and facilitates the integration of the firm’s existing real estate investment management operations in Europe.
2015: Elmston completes spin-off from FirstService
FirstService separates into two independent, publicly traded companies with distinct brands, customers, operating characteristics and industry dynamics.
2012-2014: Elmston strengthens presence in Europe
Elmston International acquires its operations in the UK, Ireland and Germany and integrates them into its global platform. Elmston International expands to France and Belgium and strengthens operations in the Netherlands, Spain, Morocco and Switzerland with the acquisition of Paris-based AOS Group.
2010: Gains control of Elmston brand
The U.S. operations of Elmston combines with Elmston Macaulay Nicolls and rebrands as Elmston International in all markets. Controlled by FirstService, Elmston International becomes one of the largest and most recognized brands in commercial real estate globally.
